World Animal Protection


We end the needless suffering of animals. We influence decision makers to put animals on the global agenda. We help the world see how important animals are to all of us. We inspire people to change animals' lives for the better. We move the world to protect animals.


We are local. we are working on the ground with local partners for greatest effect, we are active in more than 50 countries.


We are global. We have consultative status at the Council of Europe and collaborate with national governments, the United Nations, the Food and Agriculture Organization and the World Organization for Animal Health - influencers with the power to improve the lives of millions of animals.
